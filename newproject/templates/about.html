Plantation Information System With IoT (DetectGreen)

A platform that helps iGrow student to make their own research about farming using technology and to introduce Malaysian farmers to 4.0 technology.

Working process

These are steps of how to start your own research.

Channels

First, create your own channel to start making your own research. You can have as many channels as you can to start your own. You must login first using your iGrow knowledge account and click create channel to start.

IoT Sensor

To connect your sensor first you must login and then connect to this url 'ws://localhost:8000/ws/polData/' to get your result.

Discussion

You can discuss and exchange idea through comment in the specific channel that you choose.
